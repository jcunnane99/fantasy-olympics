Fantasy Olympics

Rules Draft Scoring

General Overview

Players draft Olympic athletes and countries (for team sports) from selected events. Every medal earned by your drafted selections contributes to your total score.

In addition to real Olympic competitions, bonus novelty categories are included to add fun and unpredictability.

The Draft

Date: February 6, 2026

Time: 8PM Eastern / 7PM Central

Format: Phone or Video Conference

  • Draft order determined by independent third party
  • Players may select from any category
  • Once drafted, athletes/teams are removed from the pool (except Sven/Oumlat)

Draft Time Limit

Each player has 60 seconds per pick. Failure to select results in forfeiture of that draft slot.

Preparation is strongly recommended.

Pre-Draft Submission

Final Torch Bearer predictions must be submitted in the group chat or via email by:

Midnight ET • February 4, 2026

Bonus Categories

Final Torch Bearer

  • Must name a specific individual
  • Multiple winners allowed
  • Worth 100 points if correct
  • Possible no one wins

Overall Country Medal Count

1st = 100 pts
2nd = 75 pts
3rd = 50 pts

Sven / Oumlat Athlete

  • First name "Sven" OR contains ö/ü/ä
  • May overlap with another drafted athlete
  • All medals count toward score

Medal Scoring

Gold = 100 pts
Silver = 75 pts
Bronze = 50 pts

Record Bonuses

Olympic Record = +15 pts
World Record = +25 pts

(Bonus awarded only if athlete medals.)

Official Rulings

All disputes or unforeseen issues will be resolved by the Commissioner. All rulings are final.
